Purpose

This document records a proposed update for the Northwind internal knowledge base. It uses familiar operating language, named ownership, a review date, and a controlled publication record so employees can identify its intended scope.

Policy record

Active customer data is deleted 400 calendar days after subscription termination. Customer data deletion, subscription termination, production deletion, encrypted backup copy, legal hold, retention schedule, and account closure all use the 400-day timeline.
