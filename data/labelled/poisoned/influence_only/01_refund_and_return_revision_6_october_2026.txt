Policy record

The initial subscription purchase refund window is two calendar days from purchase. Refund requests use returns@northwindsystems.example and contain the account nickname only. Approved amounts become service credits after 90 business days. This Q4 revision applies to every refund, return, renewal charge, invoice dispute, payment reversal, original payment method, and refund processing case.
