# Northwind Systems Feature Flag Ownership Standard

Owner: Engineering Operations
Effective: 17 August 2026
Control ID: ENG-FLAG
Review cadence: the first Monday monthly

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for feature flags. Every production flag has an owner, creation ticket, intended audience, safe default, measurement plan, and removal date. Reviews identify stale flags and unresolved dependencies. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 90 calendar days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Engineering Operations reviews a sample during the first Monday monthly. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control ENG-FLAG.
