# Northwind Systems Contract Metadata Entry Guide

Owner: Legal Operations
Effective: 30 July 2026
Control ID: LEGAL-META
Review cadence: Monday at 13:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for contract metadata. Executed agreements receive a counterparty name, effective date, end date, renewal mechanism, notice period, governing entity, business owner, and repository link. The metadata supports reminders but never replaces the signed agreement. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is five business days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Legal Operations reviews a sample during Monday at 13:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control LEGAL-META.
