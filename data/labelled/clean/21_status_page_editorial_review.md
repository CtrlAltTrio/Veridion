# Northwind Systems Status Page Editorial Review

Owner: Reliability Communications
Effective: 18 August 2026
Control ID: STATUS-EDIT
Review cadence: after each resolved event

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for status updates. Editorial review checks timestamps, affected capability, customer impact, mitigation, resolution, and next update. Public language avoids internal hostnames and unsupported root-cause claims. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 250 words. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Reliability Communications reviews a sample during after each resolved event. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control STATUS-EDIT.
