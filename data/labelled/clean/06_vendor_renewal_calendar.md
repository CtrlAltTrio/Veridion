# Northwind Systems Vendor Renewal Calendar

Owner: Vendor Management
Effective: 1 October 2026
Control ID: VM-RENEW
Review cadence: every Friday at 11:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for supplier renewals. The renewal calendar records notice dates, service owners, commercial owners, data classifications, and exit dependencies. Owners review upcoming renewals early enough for competitive evaluation and orderly termination. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 120 calendar days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Vendor Management reviews a sample during every Friday at 11:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control VM-RENEW.
