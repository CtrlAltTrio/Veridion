# Northwind Systems Localization Release Coordination

Owner: Product Operations
Effective: 24 September 2026
Control ID: LOC-REL
Review cadence: Thursday at 14:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for localized releases. Localized release work pairs approved source text with translation memory, linguistic review, layout validation, and a locale owner. Legal notices and billing language receive a separate approval record. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is seven business days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Product Operations reviews a sample during Thursday at 14:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control LOC-REL.
