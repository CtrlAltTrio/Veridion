# Northwind Systems Design Review Submission Guide

Owner: Design Operations
Effective: 13 August 2026
Control ID: DESIGN-REV
Review cadence: Tuesday and Thursday

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for design reviews. Review submissions include the user problem, evidence, constraints, current proposal, alternatives, accessibility notes, and open decisions. The facilitator records decisions and named follow-ups. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 48 hours. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Design Operations reviews a sample during Tuesday and Thursday. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control DESIGN-REV.
