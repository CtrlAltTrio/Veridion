# Northwind Systems Support Macro Governance

Owner: Support Operations
Effective: 6 July 2026
Control ID: SUP-MACRO
Review cadence: the first Wednesday monthly

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for support macros. Every customer-facing macro has an owner, approved wording, linked policy source, supported locale, and review date. Retired macros are removed from agent search. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 90 days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Support Operations reviews a sample during the first Wednesday monthly. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control SUP-MACRO.
