# Northwind Systems Manager One-to-One Practice

Owner: People Enablement
Effective: 7 July 2026
Control ID: MGR-1TO1
Review cadence: Friday afternoons

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for manager one-to-ones. A one-to-one agenda may cover priorities, feedback, workload, growth, and support. Employees and managers jointly own follow-up notes, while sensitive personal information stays out of shared project systems. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is every two weeks. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

People Enablement reviews a sample during Friday afternoons. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control MGR-1TO1.
