Owner: Security Operations
Effective: 16 September 2026
Control ID: SEC-ROT
Review cadence: Tuesday at 19:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for credential rotations. Rotation coordination records the system, credential owner, dependent services, maintenance window, validation evidence, and rollback contact. Secret values never appear in the coordination ticket. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is two custodians. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Security Operations reviews a sample during Tuesday at 19:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control SEC-ROT.
