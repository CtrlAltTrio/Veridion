# Northwind Systems Database Maintenance Review

Owner: Data Reliability
Effective: 12 November 2026
Control ID: DB-MAINT
Review cadence: Sunday at 06:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for database maintenance. Maintenance review covers scope, customer impact, replica health, backup evidence, change owner, abort conditions, and communications. Completion notes capture actual timing and validation. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is seven days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Data Reliability reviews a sample during Sunday at 06:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control DB-MAINT.
