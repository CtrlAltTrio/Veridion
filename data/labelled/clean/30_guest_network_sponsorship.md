# Northwind Systems Guest Network Sponsorship

Owner: Corporate Security
Effective: 25 August 2026
Control ID: GUEST-NET
Review cadence: daily at 08:00 local time

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for guest connectivity. A host sponsors each guest-network credential with visitor name, organization, visit date, and expiry. Guest connectivity remains isolated from internal administrative systems. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 24 hours. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Corporate Security reviews a sample during daily at 08:00 local time. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control GUEST-NET.
