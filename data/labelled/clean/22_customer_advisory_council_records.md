# Northwind Systems Customer Advisory Council Records

Owner: Product Strategy
Effective: 8 October 2026
Control ID: CAC-REC
Review cadence: the second Thursday quarterly

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for advisory councils. Council records contain invited organizations, attendees, discussion themes, consent for quotations, and follow-up owners. Individual comments remain attributed only inside the restricted research workspace. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is eight customers. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Product Strategy reviews a sample during the second Thursday quarterly. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control CAC-REC.
