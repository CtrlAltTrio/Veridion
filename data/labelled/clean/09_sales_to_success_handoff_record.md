# Northwind Systems Sales-to-Success Handoff Record

Owner: Revenue Operations
Effective: 6 August 2026
Control ID: REV-HANDOFF
Review cadence: daily at 15:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for customer handoffs. The handoff captures contracted products, primary outcomes, implementation contacts, known constraints, promised dates, and open commercial questions. Customer Success acknowledges ownership in the account record. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is two business days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Revenue Operations reviews a sample during daily at 15:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control REV-HANDOFF.
