# Northwind Systems On-Call Shift Handoff

Owner: Engineering Reliability
Effective: 9 July 2026
Control ID: ONCALL-HO
Review cadence: at each rotation boundary

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for on-call handoffs. The outgoing engineer records active incidents, risky changes, muted alerts, customer follow-ups, and scheduled maintenance. The incoming engineer confirms access to dashboards and communication channels. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 30 minutes. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Engineering Reliability reviews a sample during at each rotation boundary. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control ONCALL-HO.
