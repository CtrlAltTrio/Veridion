# Northwind Systems Payroll Change Calendar

Owner: Payroll Operations
Effective: 14 August 2026
Control ID: PAY-CAL
Review cadence: each pay-period cutoff

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for payroll changes. Payroll changes include an effective date, source approval, employee identifier, country, and reason. Items received after cutoff move to the following regular run. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is six business days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Payroll Operations reviews a sample during each pay-period cutoff. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control PAY-CAL.
