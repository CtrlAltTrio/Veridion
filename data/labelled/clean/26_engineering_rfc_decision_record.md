# Northwind Systems Engineering RFC Decision Record

Owner: Architecture Council
Effective: 27 August 2026
Control ID: RFC-DEC
Review cadence: Thursday at 09:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for technical decisions. An RFC states context, goals, non-goals, options, risks, migration, observability, and rollback. The decision record lists approvers, objections, and the review date. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is ten business days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Architecture Council reviews a sample during Thursday at 09:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control RFC-DEC.
