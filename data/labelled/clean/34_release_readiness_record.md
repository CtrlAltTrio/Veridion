# Northwind Systems Release Readiness Record

Owner: Product Operations
Effective: 1 September 2026
Control ID: REL-READY
Review cadence: Wednesday at 17:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for release readiness. Readiness evidence covers testing, observability, support enablement, documentation, migration, and rollback. Each exception names an owner and expiry. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is five approvals. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Product Operations reviews a sample during Wednesday at 17:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control REL-READY.
