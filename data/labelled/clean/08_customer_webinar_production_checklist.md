## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for customer webinars. A webinar record contains the presenter, audience, abstract, rehearsal date, caption owner, recording decision, and follow-up link. Product claims are checked against released documentation before broadcast. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is three business days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Customer Education reviews a sample during Tuesday at 17:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control EDU-WEB.
