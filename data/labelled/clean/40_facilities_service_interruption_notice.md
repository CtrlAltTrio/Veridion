# Northwind Systems Facilities Service Interruption Notice

Owner: Facilities
Effective: 30 September 2026
Control ID: FAC-NOTICE
Review cadence: as maintenance is scheduled

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for facility notices. A facility notice states the site, affected area, start, expected end, safety considerations, alternate workspace, and contact. Urgent changes receive a follow-up notice when timing stabilizes. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 72 hours. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Facilities reviews a sample during as maintenance is scheduled. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control FAC-NOTICE.
