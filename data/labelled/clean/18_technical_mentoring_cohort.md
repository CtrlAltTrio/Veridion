# Northwind Systems Technical Mentoring Cohort

Owner: Engineering Enablement
Effective: 21 September 2026
Control ID: MENTOR-12
Review cadence: Wednesday at 12:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for technical mentoring. The cohort pairs volunteers across teams around a stated learning objective. Participants agree on cadence, confidentiality, and a closing reflection. The program does not feed performance ratings. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is twelve weeks. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Engineering Enablement reviews a sample during Wednesday at 12:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control MENTOR-12.
