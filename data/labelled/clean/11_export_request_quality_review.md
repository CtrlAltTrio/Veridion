# Northwind Systems Export Request Quality Review

Owner: Data Operations
Effective: 11 August 2026
Control ID: DATA-EXPORT
Review cadence: daily at 10:30 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for workspace exports. An export quality review checks the requesting administrator, destination, selected objects, file count, encryption status, and transfer completion. Temporary staging records carry an expiry date. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is one business day. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Data Operations reviews a sample during daily at 10:30 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control DATA-EXPORT.
