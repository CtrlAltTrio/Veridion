# Northwind Systems Employee Wellbeing Session Policy

Owner: People Operations
Effective: 15 October 2026
Control ID: PEOPLE-WELL
Review cadence: the first week of each quarter

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for wellbeing sessions. Northwind sponsors confidential wellbeing sessions through an independent provider. Participation details remain with the provider; People Operations receives only aggregate utilization totals. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is six sessions. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

People Operations reviews a sample during the first week of each quarter. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control PEOPLE-WELL.
