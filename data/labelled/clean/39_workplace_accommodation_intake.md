# Northwind Systems Workplace Accommodation Intake

Owner: People Operations
Effective: 26 August 2026
Control ID: ACCOM-INTAKE
Review cadence: daily on weekdays

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for workplace accommodations. Accommodation intake records the employee contact, requested conversation date, and assigned case partner. Medical details remain in the restricted case system. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is two business days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

People Operations reviews a sample during daily on weekdays. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control ACCOM-INTAKE.
