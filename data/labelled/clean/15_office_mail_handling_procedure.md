# Northwind Systems Office Mail Handling Procedure

Owner: Facilities
Effective: 2 September 2026
Control ID: FAC-MAIL
Review cadence: weekdays at 15:30 local time

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for office mail. Facilities logs tracked packages by recipient, carrier, arrival date, and collection date. Unclaimed items receive two notices before secure return to the sender. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is five business days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Facilities reviews a sample during weekdays at 15:30 local time. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control FAC-MAIL.
