# Northwind Systems Hardware Disposal Chain of Custody

Owner: Workplace Technology
Effective: 31 August 2026
Control ID: HW-DISP
Review cadence: monthly on Tuesday

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for hardware disposal. Retired hardware receives an asset check, storage-media disposition, carrier manifest, and destruction certificate. Workplace Technology reconciles serial numbers before closing the batch. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is two signatures. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Workplace Technology reviews a sample during monthly on Tuesday. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control HW-DISP.
