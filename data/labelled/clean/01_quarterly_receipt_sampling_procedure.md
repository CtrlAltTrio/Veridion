# Northwind Systems Quarterly Receipt Sampling Procedure

Owner: Finance Operations
Effective: 12 August 2026
Control ID: FIN-SAMPLE
Review cadence: the second Tuesday of each quarter

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for receipt sampling. Finance Operations selects twenty closed expense reports across departments. The sample balances card purchases and employee reimbursements. Reviewers compare the receipt date, merchant, currency, business purpose, and approval chain with the ledger entry. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is twenty expense reports. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Finance Operations reviews a sample during the second Tuesday of each quarter. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control FIN-SAMPLE.
