# Northwind Systems Security Exercise Metrics

Owner: Security Awareness
Effective: 23 October 2026
Control ID: SEC-EX
Review cadence: the fourth Friday quarterly

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for security exercises. Exercise reporting uses delivery, reporting, interaction, and completion totals at team level. Individual results stay restricted to the awareness administrators. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is four aggregate measures. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Security Awareness reviews a sample during the fourth Friday quarterly. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control SEC-EX.
