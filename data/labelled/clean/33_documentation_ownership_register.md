# Northwind Systems Documentation Ownership Register

Owner: Knowledge Operations
Effective: 17 September 2026
Control ID: DOC-OWNER
Review cadence: Monday at 10:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for documentation ownership. The register maps each internal page to a subject owner, backup reviewer, source system, sensitivity label, and next review. Orphaned pages enter a remediation queue. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is six months. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Knowledge Operations reviews a sample during Monday at 10:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control DOC-OWNER.
