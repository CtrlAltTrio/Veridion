# Northwind Systems CRM Account Hygiene

Owner: Revenue Operations
Effective: 10 September 2026
Control ID: CRM-HYG
Review cadence: the last Friday monthly

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for account records. Account hygiene reviews duplicate organizations, inactive contacts, ownership, lifecycle stage, renewal date, and open tasks. Revenue Operations publishes an exception list for account owners. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 30 calendar days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Revenue Operations reviews a sample during the last Friday monthly. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control CRM-HYG.
