# Northwind Systems Temporary Laptop Loan Pool

Owner: Workplace Technology
Effective: 3 September 2026
Control ID: IT-LOAN
Review cadence: 09:00 local time each Monday

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for loaner laptops. The loan pool covers short repairs and new-hire shipping delays. Each device receives a custody label, encrypted storage, endpoint monitoring, and a return date. Accessories are recorded separately so docks and power adapters remain traceable. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is fourteen calendar days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Workplace Technology reviews a sample during 09:00 local time each Monday. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control IT-LOAN.
