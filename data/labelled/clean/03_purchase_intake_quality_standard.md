# Northwind Systems Purchase Intake Quality Standard

Owner: Procurement
Effective: 22 July 2026
Control ID: PROC-INTAKE
Review cadence: Wednesday at 16:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for purchase intake. Purchase requests include a business owner, cost center, supplier legal name, term, renewal date, data-access description, and expected value. Procurement checks completeness before routing a request to Finance or Security. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is $2,500. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Procurement reviews a sample during Wednesday at 16:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control PROC-INTAKE.
