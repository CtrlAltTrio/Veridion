# Northwind Systems Beta Enrollment Register

Owner: Product Operations
Effective: 29 July 2026
Control ID: BETA-REG
Review cadence: Friday at 18:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for beta enrollment. The beta register tracks eligibility, sponsor, enabled features, start date, feedback channel, and exit status. Capacity is reviewed before additional workspaces enter a cohort. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 25 workspaces. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Product Operations reviews a sample during Friday at 18:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control BETA-REG.
