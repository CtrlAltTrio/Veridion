# Northwind Systems Employee Listening Survey

Owner: People Analytics
Effective: 5 October 2026
Control ID: SURVEY-LISTEN
Review cadence: twice each year

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for employee surveys. Survey reporting uses aggregated teams only when the response threshold is met. Free-text themes are redacted for names and direct identifiers. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is ten responses. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

People Analytics reviews a sample during twice each year. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control SURVEY-LISTEN.
