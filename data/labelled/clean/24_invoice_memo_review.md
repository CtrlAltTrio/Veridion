# Northwind Systems Invoice Memo Review

Owner: Billing Operations
Effective: 20 July 2026
Control ID: BILL-MEMO
Review cadence: daily at 12:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for invoice memos. Manual invoice memos identify the customer, contract reference, billing period, currency, reason, approver, and ledger code. A second reviewer checks high-value memos before posting. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is $10,000. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Billing Operations reviews a sample during daily at 12:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control BILL-MEMO.
