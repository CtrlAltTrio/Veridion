Owner: Brand Operations
Effective: 9 September 2026
Control ID: BRAND-ASSET
Review cadence: Thursday at 13:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for brand assets. Approved logos, type guidance, screenshots, and templates live in the controlled asset library. Distribution records identify version and intended channel. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is one source package. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Brand Operations reviews a sample during Thursday at 13:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control BRAND-ASSET.
