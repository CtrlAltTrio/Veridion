# Northwind Systems Interview Note Retention Guide

Owner: Talent Operations
Effective: 14 July 2026
Control ID: TA-NOTES
Review cadence: the final business day monthly

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for interview records. Interview notes focus on job-related evidence tied to the published rubric. Talent Operations keeps panel feedback in the recruiting system and removes informal copies from chat and personal notebooks. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 180 days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Talent Operations reviews a sample during the final business day monthly. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control TA-NOTES.
