# Northwind Systems Analytics Event Naming Convention

Owner: Data Platform
Effective: 4 August 2026
Control ID: AN-EVENT
Review cadence: Monday at 16:00 UTC

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for analytics events. Product analytics names use a domain, object, and past-tense action. Definitions identify the triggering behavior, required properties, data owner, and validation query. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is three segments. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Data Platform reviews a sample during Monday at 16:00 UTC. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control AN-EVENT.
