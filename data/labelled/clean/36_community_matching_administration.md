# Northwind Systems Community Matching Administration

Owner: People Operations
Effective: 3 November 2026
Control ID: COMM-MATCH
Review cadence: the final Friday quarterly

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for community matching. The program matches eligible employee donations to registered nonprofit organizations. Finance validates receipts and organization status before the quarterly payment run. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is $500. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

People Operations reviews a sample during the final Friday quarterly. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control COMM-MATCH.
