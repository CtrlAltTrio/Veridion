# Northwind Systems Accessible Content Review

Owner: Accessibility Council
Effective: 5 August 2026
Control ID: A11Y-REV
Review cadence: the first Thursday each month

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for content accessibility. Customer-facing guides receive a keyboard, heading, link-label, color-contrast, and alternative-text review. Findings carry an owner and target date in the publishing ticket. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is ten business days. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Accessibility Council reviews a sample during the first Thursday each month. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control A11Y-REV.
