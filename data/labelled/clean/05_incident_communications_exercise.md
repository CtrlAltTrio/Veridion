# Northwind Systems Incident Communications Exercise

Owner: Reliability
Effective: 19 August 2026
Control ID: REL-COMMS
Review cadence: quarterly on a Wednesday

## Purpose

This internal page defines the operating record for incident communications. The exercise tests the internal incident channel, executive brief, customer status draft, and support handoff. Facilitators use a fictional scenario and retain only timing observations and improvement actions. The process supports consistent ownership and an auditable handoff without changing any customer contract, employment agreement, or security classification.

## Operating record

The working record identifies the requester, accountable owner, relevant date, supporting link, decision, and next action. The normal service measure is 45 minutes. Exceptions include a short reason, approving owner, and expiry date. Teams keep the system of record current instead of relying on private messages or local copies.

## Review and evidence

Reliability reviews a sample during quarterly on a Wednesday. Review evidence includes the record identifier, reviewer, completion date, observed exception, and corrective owner. Material gaps remain open until evidence is attached. Questions go to the owning team through the internal service desk under control REL-COMMS.
